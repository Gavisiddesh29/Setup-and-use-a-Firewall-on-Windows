#### **8. How Firewall Filters Traffic (Summary)**

* Firewalls control traffic using rules based on:

  * **Ports** (e.g., 23, 22)
  * **Protocol** (TCP/UDP)
  * **IP Address**
* **Allow** rules let traffic through; **Block** rules deny it.
* Helps protect against unauthorized access.
